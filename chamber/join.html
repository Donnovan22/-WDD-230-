Membership Level Benefits

Non Profit Membership

• Be a member of the AQP Chamber of Commerce for no fee.
• One free ticket to the Annual Awards event

Bronze Membership

• Join at this level if you want to use Chamber marketing programs to promote your business.
• Meeting with Chamber staff to strategize around Chamber resources and opportunities
• $450/year

Silver Membership

• Made for medium to large companies and non-profit organizations seeking a higher profile opportunity to market and demonstrate investment in the Superior community.
• Two free tickets to the Annual Awards event
• $1200/year

Gold Membership

• Our top membership level provides the highest visibility and indication of community investment.
• Four free tickets to the Annual Awards event
• Logo appears on Chamber website home page, banners
• Annual meeting with Executive Director and Board Officer
• $3,250/year
